We would like to express our sincere gratitude to our sponsor, Ansys, for supporting us by providing their simulation design tool, Ansys Discovery, to help us develop our car.
Ansys is an engineering simulation software company for product design, testing and operation. For more than 50 years, Ansys engineering simulation software has enabled innovators across industries to push boundaries using the predictive power of simulation. Ansys Discovery combines instant physics simulation, high-fidelity simulation and interactive geometry modeling in a easy-to-use experience, giving us highly accurate data and feedback of our car.
