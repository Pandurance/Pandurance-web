We would like to express our sincere gratitude to our sponsor, Memorigin, for supporting us financially - providing the funds for the materials we used for our pit display in the Development Class Competition.

Memorigin is a well-known Hong Kong watchmaker which specialises in creating Tourbillon watches. Being the first ever Tourbillon watch brand founded in Hong Kong, Memorigin creates watches that presents the essence of “East meets West”, blending Eastern culture with Western technology. With a commitment to the preservation of cultural heritage and the development of product and technology, Memorigin is now a recognised as a reputed brand in the international market, spreading the crux of the “East meets West” spirit to all parts of the world.
